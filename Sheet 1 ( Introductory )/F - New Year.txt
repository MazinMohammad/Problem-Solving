F - New Year

Problem Statement
How many hours do we have until New Year at MM o'clock (24-hour notation) on 3030th, December?

Constraints
1≤M≤231≤M≤23
MM is an integer.
Input
Input is given from Standard Input in the following format:

MM
Output
If we have xx hours until New Year at MM o'clock on 3030th, December, print xx.

Sample 1
Inputcopy	Outputcopy
21
27
We have 2727 hours until New Year at 2121 o'clock on 3030th, December.

Sample 2
Inputcopy	Outputcopy
12
36


Solution:

#include <iostream>
using namespace std;

int main()
{
   int M;
   cin >> M;
   cout << 48 - M;
}
